About this site

FumoFumos.com is a small site for me to share my pixel art.

All art here is made by me and is free to download and use however you like!

Have fun and fumofumo :]

What are fumos?

Fumos are plush dolls from the Touhou Project.

They are a popular meme in the community and a cute collectable.
